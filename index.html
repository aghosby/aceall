Prompt Delivery of consignment is very essential to ensure good freight forwarders/ customer good relationship. We can deliver the consignment within 48 hours within Lagos and 72 hours outside Lagos.

Our warehousing services helps guard against pilferage. We have addressed this problem by having secured warehouses in all our areas of operation. Our largest warehouse (Lagos) has about 500 by 1000 sq/m space.
