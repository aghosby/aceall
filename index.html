Ibitayo Ayomide Olubode
April 29th 1983 - January 11, 2020
Download Storybook

This memorial website was created in loving memory of a loving wife, mother, daughter, sister, aunt, and friend, with a beautiful heart sold out to Jesus. Ibitayo Ayomide Olubode, 37, born on April 29, 1983 and passed away on January 11, 2020. You are dearly loved and greatly missed. We will remember you forever.

Ibitayo Ayomide Olubode, nee Martins was born in Lagos on the 29th of April 1983 to Mr. Adelola Ibitayo Martins of Lagos Island and Mrs. Adeola Mary Martins, nee Oguntona of Awe, Oyo state. She was the third child born to this beautiful family.

She had her primary school education at Our Lady of Apostles Private School, Yaba then moved on to Queen's College Yaba, Lagos for secondary education. She was admitted into the University of Lagos to study Law and was awarded a Bachelor of Law degree, LLB in 2006, after which she went to the Nigerian Law School in Abuja from 2006 to 2007.

Aboyede Salami

We had both been pulled along with some other girls from our initial classes to form the would be notorious “T” arm. In Ayo’s very own words “Marinho joined us together in this great class”. Though we were considered a noisy bunch, some girls still stood out as genuinely good and well mannered. Your mother was one of them. She was always neat and properly dressed with nothing out of place.
